Was weaponing cycling good?

Back in DMC3 (with no mods), switching weapons was a toggle mechanic that allowed you to switch between 2 guns and two Devil Arms.
This meant that players could seamlessly switch between weapons without moving the thumbs from the face buttons.
This also saved the hassle from remebering what was your next weapon in the weapon cycle, because there was no cycle between 3 or more weapons.

Now, I am not against givin the player more than 3 weapons or more, I really do want devs to give players the option to access all weapons mid-game.
The problem that comes in games that heavily rely on a controller, is that you are very limited to the amount of buttons the players
can access and switch between with his thumbs.
I explained this thoroughly in the past that your thumbs press most of the buttons compared to your other fingers that press 1-2 buttons, i.e. the triggers and bumpers.

A player must cycle through Gilgamesh in order to equip Lucifer for the easy JC.

  • DMC3 allows you with a sinple press of a button to switch between weapons, i.e. weapon toggle
  • Doom Eternal gives you the option to assign one key to a single weapon

You just switch to the weapon, and input the attack.
Such case cannot exist in DMC4 and 5 because of this cycling.
